The Rescue Radar

Our rescue radar app is designed to provide a simple yet effective solution for identifying and responding to disasters. Users can easily upload photos of potential emergencies such as fires, floods, or earthquakes through our intuitive interface. Once uploaded, our app analyzes the images to detect various types of disasters without relying on complex algorithms, ensuring a straightforward user experience.



The Rescue Radar

Using geolocation services, our app accurately determines the location of the reported disaster, facilitating prompt response efforts. This feature enables emergency responders to swiftly navigate to the scene, reducing response time and potentially saving lives. Furthermore, our app incorporates a push notification system to alert users who are near the detected disaster, allowing them to take necessary precautions and avoid danger zones.
